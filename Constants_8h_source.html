533 // from the PDG on Monte Carlo Codes:
534 /*
535 14. Nuclear codes are given as 10-digit numbers 10LZZZAAAI.
536 For a nucleus consisting of np protons, nn neutrons and
537 n s, A = np+nn+n gives the total baryon number, Z = np
538 the total charge and L = n the total number of strange quarks.
539 I gives the isomer level, with I = 0 corresponding to the ground
540 state and I > 0 to excitations, see[8], where states denoted
541 m,n,p,q translate to I = 1 4. As examples, the deuteron
542 is 1000010020 and 235U is 1000922350.
543 */
